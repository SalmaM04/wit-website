Club Mission & Vision

Women+ In Tech @ Chabot College is a student-led organization dedicated to empowering women in technology. Through networking, mentorship, workshops, and career resources, we foster an inclusive community for learning and growth. Join us to bridge the gender gap and thrive in tech!
